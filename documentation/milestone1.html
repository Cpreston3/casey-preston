PWP Milestone 1

Purpose:

With this PWP project I would like to showcase my technical skills in a portfolio.

Audience:

The target audience for this project is the IT community, also including small business and friends.

Goals:

To obtain a full-time developer position.

User Persona

Steffen Wienberg

DEMOGRAPHICS

  • NAME: Steffen Wienberg
  • GENDER: Male
  • AGE: 48
  • JOB: Job Recruiter

TECHNOLOGIES:

  • Desk top
  • mobile device
  • laptop
  • Social Networking

"As an user, I am looking for a site that will allow me to see all the qualities my company needs."

Behaviors:

Steffen is a recruiter, HE spends most of time at a desk looking at social media platforms like Facebook, LinkedIn, and Twitter for new people to recruit. He sees a lot of resumes and skips the ones that don't catch his eye. He has influence over many companies and is a well respected for his good judgement.

Attitude:

Steffen is a extrovert which is why he is so good at his job. He loves what he does and he works more than 60 hours a week. Steffen is single so he can focus on his career. When he is not working he is usually attending some social event to do more networking fro potential clients. There is a certain type of personality that he searches for that other recruiter seem to miss which gives him an advantage.

Goals:

Steffen wants a resume that stands out with easy to read skills. He wants to spend no more than 5 minutes on a potential candidate. Steffens want to skim the information and wants to be able to contact potential candidates immediately, if the website is unable to provide those services for Steffen he moves on to the next candidate.

Frustrations:

Steffen wants to hire someone with 1-2 years experience at an entry level salary. He wants someone with that is trainable and willing to work hard. Steffen does not like to waste time so he also wants someone reliable and that can do the work expected of them. His contacts are very important to him so, the people that he selects have already been filtered by Steffen's standards.
